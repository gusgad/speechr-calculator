How to use

Thanks for using our Speech Recognition enabled calculator!

The usage of calculator is easy and straightforward. To calculate, you just need to press "Record" button. As soon as it is pressed, the calculator is listening for numbers and operators, in other word for a statement to calculate (example: "Ten plus eight"). NOTE: Calculator evaluates statements with multiplications and divisions as a whole, that means if you say "Ten plus eight multiply two" - it will multiply eight by two first and then add ten. To clear the results and stop the speech recognition - press "AC". It is possible to change the color scheme and font size in "Settings" menu. Phrases for operators: "PLUS" for Addition, "MINUS" for Subtraction, "MULTIPLY" for Multiplication, "SLASH" for Division (the word "DIVIDE" will not work).
